Associate Product Director & Senior Architect

Healthcare technology leader with 25+ years delivering mission-critical systems across New Zealand, Canada, Australia, and the United States. I work at the intersection of product strategy and technical delivery — from defining EHR and HIE solutions for provincial and state governments, to leading ML/AI platform initiatives on AWS at scale.

What I'm Thinking About Now

Healthcare AI is moving fast — but most of the hard problems aren't about the models. They're about the engineering required to make them actually work in production.

The part that interests me most is the challenge of taking a model from prototype to production in a regulated healthcare environment — the infrastructure, the security architecture, the operational patterns that make it work at scale. Getting from "we have a model" to a secure, compliant, observable system running in production is genuinely hard, and that's exactly the kind of problem I enjoy.
